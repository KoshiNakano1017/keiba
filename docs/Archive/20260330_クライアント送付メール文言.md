# Client Email Templates and Guidance

## Email Template for Sending Requirements Documents

Subject: Requirements Documents for Review

Dear Shibata-san,

I hope this message finds you well. 

Attached, please find the requirements documents we have prepared based on our discussions. I would appreciate it if you could take some time to review them at your earliest convenience.

### Confirmation Items
- Are all requirements accurately captured?
- Is there any information that requires further clarification?
- Do you foresee any potential issues with the proposed timeline?

### Next Steps
1. Please send any feedback or questions you might have by [insert due date].
2. We can schedule a follow-up meeting to discuss your comments and finalize the documentation.
3. Once we have your confirmation, we will proceed with the next phase of the project.

Thank you for your attention to these documents. I look forward to your response.

Best regards,
[Your Name]  
[Your Position]  
[Your Contact Information]  

## Additional Guidance
- Make sure to personalize the email with your specific details.
- Attach relevant documents before sending the email.
- Consider providing a brief overview of the contents of the attached documents in the body of the email.
